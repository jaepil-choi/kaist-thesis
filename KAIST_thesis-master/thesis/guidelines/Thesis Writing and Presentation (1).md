# **The format of the thesis**

´ü« Follow the format of the _Journal of Finance_ closely.


´ü«
Tables and figures


´éí
You should not bring up the tables from the output of a statistical
package directly. You should make up your tables for your
thesis.


´éí
You should not use any vertical lines in tables, and minimize the
usage of horizontal lines.


´éí
Table and figures should be self-contained. That is, they should
contain all the descriptions of the variables, estimation method,
and sample period.
