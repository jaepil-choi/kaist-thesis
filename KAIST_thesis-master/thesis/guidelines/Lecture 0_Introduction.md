# **Picking up a Thesis Topic**

’ü«
First, you need to ask yourself what area you are interested in.


’éĪ
Since you are writing a thesis for the financial engineering
MasterŌĆÖs degree, the topic should be closely related to the
finance area.


’ü«
If the broad area for the thesis is decided, then you need to look over
the existing literature in the area, and how the other people have
thought of the issues in the area.


’ü«
Next, you need to fix a concrete question you want to deliver in your
thesis.


’ü«
Find a key paper (or papers) for the question, and then replicate the
paper with your own data. Then think about how to adjust/expand the
key paper to the one that suits well with your idea or data.
